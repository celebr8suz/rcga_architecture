Aviation

RCGA has assisted in the design and refurbishment of the interior main cabin of a 1999 Company Aircraft. The interior was completely stripped to fuselage, with a new sound deadening/insulation lining installed, red oak paneling, hand-stitched Italian leather and gold trim. Gold trim also provided finish to the aft banquet and 8 place cabin seating configuration. The aft Lavatory contertops and walls are finished in granite. New Led task, running and emergency lights were installed throughout. Aircell Axxess SATCOM and Airshow 4000 Entertainment Systems were introduced with 17' Forward and Aft bulkhead LCD monitors and 7" personal LCD monitors.
